Per 30 TAC §290.46(j)(4) — upon inspection of the private water distribution facilities connected to the public water supply, mark each item below.
1
No direct or indirect connection exists between the public water supply and a potential source of contamination — sources of contamination are isolated by an air gap or an approved backflow prevention assembly.
2
No cross-connection exists between the public water supply and a private water system. Where an air gap is not maintained, an approved reduced-pressure-principle backflow assembly is installed.
3
No connection exists that would allow water used for condensing, cooling, or industrial processes to return to the public water supply.
4
No pipe or pipe fitting containing more than 8.0% lead exists in private water distribution facilities installed on or after 7/1/1988 and prior to 1/4/2014.
